Meet the Team

I've been working in VFX and CGI since 2008, starting with freelance projects before moving on to major feature films and TV series. Over the years, I've worked as a CG generalist, FX technical director, and VFX supervisor, taking on roles from artist to lead and supervisor. Some of the projects I've contributed to include (but not limited to) Disney's Aladdin and Disenchanted, Prehistoric Planet, News of the world, The Boys, Watchmen, The Wheel Of Time, Fast X, and a lot more.

In addition to my VFX career, I've also directed several short films, combining my technical expertise with a passion for storytelling. I enjoy pushing the boundaries of procedural workflows and cinematic world-building, always looking for ways to refine and optimize the creative process.

Companies I've worked at:

  • Lead FX Technical Director @ MPC
  • Lead FX Technical Director / Supervisor @ Ghost VFX
  • Lead FX Technical Director / Supervisor @ Outpost VFX
  • FX Technical Director @ One Of Us
  • CG Generalist @ Storyline Studio
