Meet the Team

Ali Al-Tobi

Ali Al-Tobi is a Norwegian director, VFX supervisor, and technical director with over 15 years of experience in the visual effects industry. His career spans major feature films and TV series, where he has evolved from a CG generalist to leadership roles in some of the industry's most prestigious studios.

His contributions can be seen in numerous high-profile projects, including Disney's Aladdin and Disenchanted, Prehistoric Planet, News of the World, The Boys, Watchmen, The Wheel of Time, and Fast X, among others. As a director, he combines his technical expertise with a passion for storytelling, creating compelling narratives that push the boundaries of visual storytelling.

Specializing in procedural workflows and cinematic world-building, Ali continuously refines and optimizes creative processes, bringing a unique blend of technical innovation and artistic vision to each project.
